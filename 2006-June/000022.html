Thomas Mayer wrote:
> Hello,
> 
> I'm new to this list and just wanted to ask about the status of German
> translations for ccPublisher and mozCC, as I am willing to contribute to
> that.

I've turned on the German translation for ccPublisher.  You can find it
at http://translate.creativecommons.org/de_DE/ccpublisher/.  You can
find instructions on using the new web-based system at
http://wiki.creativecommons.org/Translating_CC_Tools.

MozCC is sort of in a weird state right now.  We're working on a major
revision as part of a Summer of Code project.  When we ship that we'll
be including internationalization support.  We'll be announcing a call
for translators on this list and on the creativecommons.org weblog when
that's ready to go.

Let me know if you have any questions.
