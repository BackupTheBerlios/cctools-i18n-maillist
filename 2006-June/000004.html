As I stated in my previous emails, we will be releasing ccPublisher 2.2
with internationalization support at the iSummit later this month.  We
would like to ship ccPublisher 2.2 with as many translations as
possible.  In order to be included in ccPublisher 2.2, you must submit
your translation files by 1200 EDT (1600 UTC) on 20 June 2006.

You may submit your translation by emailing the files to the
cctools-i18n list or (preferably) by opening an issue in the ccPublisher
Tracker (http://roundup.creativecommons.org/ccpublisher).  Full details
on translation procedures are available at
http://wiki.creativecommons.org/Translating_ccPublisher.

If your translation is incomplete by the deadline, we still welcome the
contribution.  We will roll additional translations into future releases
as they are available.

Questions, comments, etc can be sent to myself or the cctools-i18n list
(http://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/cctools-i18n).

Thanks for your help,

Nathan
