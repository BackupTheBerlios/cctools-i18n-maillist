I've just uploaded ccPublisher 2.1.2, the latest i18n beta.  This
release fixes a handful of problems reported from 2.1.1 and adds two
strings to the catalogs.  There are still some known issues, recorded in
the tracker (http://roundup.creativecommons.org/ccpublisher).  In
particular, we are aware that the background for the text of Mac OS X
looks wrong, and that the window must be manually resized before the
layout is correct.  This will obviously be fixed for the final 2.2 release.

Download links available at http://wiki.creativecommons.org/CcPublisher_2_2.
